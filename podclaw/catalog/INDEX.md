## Pricing Formula

```
Total Cost = Base Cost (Premium) + EU Shipping (1st item)
Sale Price = Total Cost / (1 - Target Margin)
Gross Profit = Sale Price - Total Cost
```

- **Minimum margin**: 40% (no exceptions)
- **Pricing plan**: Printify Premium (~20% discount included)
- **Currency**: EUR (USD→EUR: 0.92)
- **VAT**: Not included in base costs (varies by country)
