## General Rules

- All canvases are in pixels at 300 DPI
- Transparent PNG-24 required (no background)
- Safe zone: 5% margin from all edges
- Text minimum: 2% of canvas height
- Position y-offset for front: typically 0.45 (slightly above center)
- Position x: 0.5 (centered horizontally)
- Scale: 1.0 (full size) unless design is intentionally small (e.g., left-chest logo)

## Left-Chest Placement Guide

For small logo/emblem designs on tees and hoodies:
- **Position**: x: 0.28, y: 0.22
- **Scale**: 0.25–0.35
- **Max design size**: ~25% of canvas width
- Works on: BP6, BP12, BP145, BP454, BP77, BP49, BP80
